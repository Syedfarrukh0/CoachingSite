About ECAT

Engineering College Admission Test (ECAT) is a test conducted in the Punjab, Pakistan each year for admission in BE/BS/BSc Engineering degree. The ECAT score counts heavily towards the admission as each student’s cumulative score is prepared with a 50% weightage to the test score and 50% to the Inter result.

ECAT is taken as multiple choice questions (MCQ). Each MCQ has 4 marks while there is a negative marking of -1 mark for wrong answer.

Test duration for ECAT is 1 hour, and it includes 100 Questions – 30 each on Mathematics, Chemistry and Physics, and 10 on Basic English.

Students who prepare for the ECAT are also prepared to appear in other private sector Engineering Colleges such as NED, Fast, and Nust.

About the Test

The entrance test comprises sections in Inter Level Mathematics, English (Antonyms, Synonyms, Analogies and Reading comprehension), Inter Level Physics and a section on IQ.
