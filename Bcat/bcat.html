About BCAT

Business College Admission Test (BCAT) is a test conducted in Sindh Testing Service (STS) , Pakistan each year for admission in BBA Or others Business degree. The BCAT score counts heavily towards the admission as each studentâ€™s cumulative score is prepared with a 40% weightage to the test score and 40% to the Inter result.

BCAT is taken as multiple choice questions (MCQ).

Test duration for BCAT is 1 hour.

Students who prepare for the BCAT are also prepared to appear in other private sector Business Colleges such as IBA, LUMS, and KUBS.

About the Test

The test has questions on Accounting, Economics, Statistics & English

Teaching Methodology

For preparation at Education's Valley , expert teachers are assigned classes to ensure quality education. Classes take place six days a week and each subject is allocated two hours on alternate days. These classes include lectures, tests based on those lectures and answers with explanations of the tests. At regular intervals, full-length tests are conducted to assess preparation levels. At Education's Valley we focus on motivating students by making education fun, yet competitive.

The BCAT course is conducted in a classroom format. Students from different walks of life come together and work as a group towards a single goal. Our environment facilitates learning via teacher-student interaction, study groups and individual attention for weaker students.
